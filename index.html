Generate Team Design Pattern for Human-Agent Collaboration

This website is created for the purpose of a master thesis at the Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU). The name of the thesis is Developing a Modelling Language for Team Design Patterns to Represent Human-Agent Collaboration.

Purpose: Provide a user-agnostic modelling language to describe Human-Agent Collaboration to facilitate the comprehension for diverse user groups.

Instructions: After clicking on "Select Dimensions", certain questions related to Human-Agent Collaboration appear. Then, you answer these questions based on what use case of Human-Agent Collaboration you wish to represent. Consequently, a corresponding Team Design Pattern to represent Human-Agent Collaboration is generated in the grey rectangle on the right side. Furthermore, you are also able to download the generated Team Design Pattern in a .svg file when clicking on "Download .svg".
